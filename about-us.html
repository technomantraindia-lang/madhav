ABOUT US

Performance You Can Trust.

Madhav Polymers is a trusted manufacturer of PVC Compounds, RP (Recycle PVC) Compounds, and PVC Masterbatches. Founded by Mr. Sunil Mishra, with his 25+ years of experience in the PVC compounding industry, delivering high-performance compounds for the wire, cable, plug, cord, and electrical sectors.

With manufacturing facilities in Delhi and Gujarat, we ensure fast production and timely delivery across India. Our products are lab tested, manufactured under strict quality control, and continuously improved through innovation to deliver consistent performance and reliability.

At Madhav Polymers, our commitment is simple—quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
